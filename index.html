Our Story

Built by a Mom, for Families

Ellen Devine is a military spouse who moved to Stafford in 2024. She quickly noticed something missing: a comfortable indoor space where families could gather, kids could play, and parents could actually connect with each other.

Working with the University of Mary Washington's Small Business Development Center, Ellen turned that idea into Silly Goose Imagination Village, a thoughtfully designed pretend village right off Route 610.

"Being a mom can feel lonely sometimes. I wanted to make a space where someone else will be there who can relate to you."

Next door to The Grounds Coffeehouse, parents can grab a coffee while watching their kids' imaginations come to life. It's a village for them AND a village for you.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages is Silly Goose designed for?

The village is designed for children from crawling infants through age 8. Older kids (9+) are welcome at a reduced rate of $5 and can enjoy the space as long as they respect the younger children's play. Table-top games are also available in the seating area for older siblings.

What is Preschool Time?

Tuesday through Friday from 9:00 to 11:00 AM, the village is reserved exclusively for children ages 0-4. This gives your littlest ones a calmer, safer environment to explore without bigger kids around. Regular open play for all ages starts at 11:00 AM.

Do I need a reservation for open play?

No reservation is needed for open play. Walk in any time during our posted hours, pay at the door, and start playing. We recommend buying tickets on our website for a faster check-in.

Can I leave my child and come back?

No. A parent or guardian must remain on the premises for the entire visit. Silly Goose is not a drop-off facility. We have comfortable seating with full visibility of the play area, and The Grounds Coffeehouse is right next door if you need a coffee.

Can I bring food or drinks?

Yes. Outside non-alcoholic food and drinks are welcome, but they must stay at the tables and seating areas, not in the play zones. Please note that we cannot accommodate severe allergies and do not guarantee an allergen-free environment.

What does a birthday party include?

Birthday parties are 2 hours with private access to the entire facility. We provide 2 staff members, plates, napkins, flatware, programming for the party, and a t-shirt for the birthday child. You bring the food, cake, and decorations. Parties are available Saturdays and Sundays, 1-3pm or 4-6pm. Members pay $295, non-members $350, for up to 15 kids.

How does the membership work?

Monthly membership is $45 for your first child, $25 for each additional sibling (ages 0-8), and $10 for siblings over 8. Members get unlimited visits, birthday party discounts, and a free snack during their child's birthday week. You can cancel anytime in person.

Where exactly is Silly Goose located?

We're at 50 N. Stafford Complex Center, Suite 108, in Stafford, VA 22556. That's off Route 610, near the Neighborhood Walmart. We're right next to The Grounds Coffeehouse. Look for Suite 108 in the complex.
