Our Story

Built by a Mom Who Saw What Stafford Was Missing

Ellen Devine is a military mom who moved to Stafford in 2024 with her family. After searching for an indoor play space where her kids could use their imaginations (not just bounce), she realized it didn't exist here.

So she built it. Silly Goose Imagination Village opened in February 2026 inside the North Stafford Complex Center, right next to The Grounds Coffeehouse. It's a space designed for pretend play, creativity, and connection.

While the kids build, imagine, and explore, parents can relax knowing their little ones are safe, supervised, and having the time of their lives.

Your Visit

How It Works

From walk-in to wind-down, here's the flow of a Silly Goose visit.

Arrive & Sign In

Walk in during open hours. Fill out the waiver (one-time) and pay at the front desk. No reservation needed for open play.

Choose Your Adventure

Kids pick their play zones freely. Imaginative stations rotate regularly so there's always something new to discover.

Play at Their Pace

No rush, no schedule. Children lead their own play. Tue-Fri 9-11am is reserved for preschoolers ages 0-4 only.

Wind Down & Go

When your little one is ready (or naptime calls), head out whenever you like. Members can come back anytime.

Common Questions

FAQ

What ages is Silly Goose designed for?

The play space is designed for children ages 0-8. Kids ages 9 and older are welcome at a reduced rate of $5. Tuesday through Friday from 9-11 AM is reserved exclusively for preschool-age children (0-4).

Do parents need to stay during the visit?

Yes. A parent or guardian must remain on-site for the entire visit. Silly Goose is not a drop-off facility. Parents can relax in the seating area, work on a laptop, or grab coffee from The Grounds Coffeehouse next door.

Can we bring outside food and drinks?

Yes, outside food and drinks are welcome. Please keep them at the designated seating areas (not in the play zones). Silly Goose cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment.

How do birthday parties work?

Parties include 2 hours of private facility access, 2 dedicated staff members, plates, napkins, flatware, programming, and a birthday t-shirt for the guest of honor. Up to 15 kids are included, with a max of 20 ($15 per additional child). Slots available Saturday and Sunday, 1-3 PM or 4-6 PM.

What does the monthly membership include?

Members get unlimited visits during open play hours, discounted party pricing ($295 vs $350), a complimentary snack during their child's birthday week, and the ability to cancel anytime in person. Additional siblings are $25/month each.

Do I need a reservation for open play?

No reservation needed. Just walk in during open hours and pay at the front desk. First-time visitors will need to fill out a one-time waiver.

Is the space safe for toddlers?

The play space is designed with young children in mind. That said, parents are responsible for supervising their children at all times. Unsafe behavior may result in being asked to leave to ensure all kids have a safe experience.

Where exactly are you located?

Silly Goose is located at 50 N. Stafford Complex Center, Suite 108, Stafford, VA 22556. We're inside the North Stafford Complex, right next to The Grounds Coffeehouse.
